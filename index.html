The Iron Skillet -------------------------------------------

DINE-IN, TAKE OUT, DELIVERY, LARGE GROUPS, PATIO SPRING TO FALL

MON-THU 11:00 - 9:00 / FRI 11:00 – 10:00 / SAT 10:00 – 10:00 / SUN 10:00 – 9:00



The Iron Skillet has been a landmark in Collingwood since Tony and Doris Sensenberger started the restaurant in 1991. They have been in the food and beverage business for 30 years, tantalizing tastebuds with new specials and serving old favourites, Schnitzels, Seafood, Pasta, Ribs, Steaks, Mexican, Thai and more.

The wine menu is extensive, there is a variety of draft beer, scotch, whiskey, vodka and tequila and customers say we have the coldest beer in town.

The licensed patio is open for the summer and it will provide an opportunity for customers to enjoy some social eatery time in the sun, while strolling around back in Collingwood to view the Waterfront.

The Iron Skillet invites you to Tantalize your Tastebuds and enjoy the quality of Tony’s food with some of the best food and service in the area.
